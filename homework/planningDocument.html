Weather Site Plan

Purpose Statement:

The purpose of this site is to give the user the current or future weather conditions in their location.

Audience Description

  • Age 30 - 60, 50,000 - 100,000 per year, College Degree.
  • They are trying to find a simple site that shows them the information they need. There is no clutter or distractions and they dont have to go looking for information.
  • They are looking for a webiste that is organized well and easy to navigate through. They want a site that has a good font and is easy to read.
  • They want a website that is an enjoyable experience on any screen size. They are looking for good pictures that show what the weather is currently like, or a live weather background so they know what it is like outside without looking at the temperature.
  • They want a website that is easy to use in a mobile environment. They also want one that is unique and fun to look at and the information is right at their fingertips.
